Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

1. What services does Tender offer?

We offer a range of services including web development, digital marketing, business consulting, and customer support. We customize our solutions to meet the specific needs of your business.

2. How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies depending on the project scope and requirements. We offer competitive rates and provide free quotes. Contact us with your project details and we will give you a customized price.

3. How long does a project typically take?

Most projects take between 2 to 4 weeks to complete. The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. We will provide you with a clear timeline before starting any work.

4. Do you offer support after project delivery?

Yes, we provide 3 months of free support for all completed projects. During this period, we assist with any issues, updates, or questions you may have. Extended support packages are also available.

5. Can you work with clients outside Kenya?

Yes, we work with clients globally. We have experience serving international clients and can adapt to different time zones and communication preferences.

6. How can I get started with Tender?

Getting started is easy. Simply visit our Contact page, fill out the form, and one of our team members will reach out to you within 24 hours. We will discuss your needs and provide a proposal.
